About

What is 444chan?

444chan by BorrowLand is a web-based service, connecting users from all over the world. We have mixed and matched the best aspects of other popular services and created our own based on these principles. Like Uber, we are encouraging you to interact with strangers and trust said strangers with your belongings. Like Tinder, we require only basic identifying information and you must be 18+, and we are not responsible if you are touched unconsensually by the strangers you meet.

As the name suggests, we draw strong inspiration from 4chan. Who exactly is this hacker, 4chan? No one knows for sure. What we do know is, the less Internet strangers know about each other, the better. We are committed to helping you maintain anonymity while enjoying the benefits of our community.
